Accommodation

Rooms, flats and housemates

Helping Nepalese in Preston find safe and affordable places to live.

For people offering rooms

  • Share area (e.g. Deepdale, City Centre, Fulwood).
  • Mention rent, bills, deposit and preferred move-in date.
  • Say if the room is single/couple, shared bathroom, etc.
  • Use private chat for sharing full address and personal details.

For people looking for rooms

  • Share budget, preferred location and if you are student/worker.
  • Tell if you are looking for short term or long term stay.
  • Be honest about your situation and respect other people‚Äôs space.
  • Do not pressure anyone for quick decisions or advance payments.
